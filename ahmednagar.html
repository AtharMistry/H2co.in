Decentralised Hydrogen Energy

Energy that is generated close to where it will be used, rather than at an industrial plant and sent through the national grid. Decentralized systems typically use renewable energy sources, like hydrogen generator. A decentralized energy system can increase security of supply, reduce transmission losses and lowers carbon emissions.
