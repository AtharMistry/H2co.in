H2 Generator Technology

Our H2 Generator product is based on advance photonics technology. Salt water is fed to the anode side (positve side) of generator. The Salt water split into its core constituents which are hydrogen ,oxygen,chlorine and sodium. The generated electron get conducted throwgh cathode side. Our technology enables the design of very compact H2 Generator ,with fast heat up and cool down in a short time.

Our H2 Generator can sustain a differential pressure without compromise. This feature supports the optimization of the compressor design with the aid of electrochemical compression as a part of the hydrogen generation. This in turn enables our electrolyzer to run at a very high current density and enhances cost-effective operation.
